About us

Welcome from the house

Every guest is welcomed with wine or champagne, fresh fruit and sweets, so the experience starts in a special way from the very first moment.

RDR Apartments is located 12 km from Curaçao Sea Aquarium and offers an outdoor pool, a garden and accommodation with air conditioning, free WiFi and a balcony.

The apartment features units with a terrace, pool view, seating area, flat-screen TV, fully equipped kitchen with a fridge and an oven, as well as a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries. Guests also have access to a microwave, a minibar and a stovetop, as well as a coffee machine and an electric kettle.

A continental, full English/Irish or Italian breakfast is available at the property.

RDR Apartments features a barbecue.

Car rental services are available at RDR Apartments.

The apartment is located 14 km from Queen Emma Bridge and 43 km from Christoffel National Park. Curaçao International Airport is 17 km away.
